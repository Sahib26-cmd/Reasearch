UPCOMING TOUR & DESTINATION

The upcoming tour, scheduled for September 2024, will take participants through a captivating journey across three historic cities: Paris, Rome, and Athens. This cultural exploration aims to immerse travelers in the rich history and vibrant traditions of each location. In Paris, participants will visit iconic landmarks such as the Eiffel Tower and the Louvre Museum, while in Rome, they will explore ancient ruins including the Colosseum and the Roman Forum. The tour will conclude in Athens, where travelers will marvel at the Acropolis and the Parthenon. This well-curated itinerary promises an enriching experience, combining historical insights with local cultural experiences in some of Europe's most storied destinations.
